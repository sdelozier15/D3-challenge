Are the Poor At-Risk?

U.S. Census Bureau and Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System research data were visualized and analyzed to explore potential correlations between health risks, age, and income. D3 Times discovers correlations between the percentage of a State's population in poverty and accessibility to healthcare.
